About us :

Iqra Anwar-ul-Quran Secondary School is a reputable and well-established educational institution dedicated to providing both Islamic and modern education along with strong moral training. The Founder and Principal is an experienced Advocate (Lawyer) with professional expertise in legal affairs and a deep passion for the field of education. He is also a skilled computer specialist and graphic designer, as well as an Islamic scholar and Hafiz-e-Quran, which allows the institution to maintain a strong balance between modern technology and religious guidance. The school follows a structured academic system with a focus on discipline, quality curriculum, and overall student development. With strong connections to the Board of Gazio-ud-Din Education, students are provided with proper admission guidance and academic support.
