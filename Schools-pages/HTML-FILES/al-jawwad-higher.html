AL JAWWAD HIGHER SEC SCHOOL

Al-Jawad Higher Secondary School & Intermediate College is a distinguished institution committed to academic excellence and character building, led by a Principal who also serves as the General Secretary of the Pakistan Private Schools & Colleges Association. With a strong educational background and a modern learning environment, the institution stands as a symbol of quality education and a bright future for its students.
